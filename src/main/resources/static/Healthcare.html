Popular Healthcare Fields

Medicine

Doctors diagnose and treat illnesses, performing surgeries and prescribing medicines. Requires an MBBS degree followed by specialization.

Nursing

Nurses provide essential patient care, assist doctors, and educate patients about health management.

Pharmacy

Pharmacists prepare and dispense medicines and advise on their proper use.

Physiotherapy

Physiotherapists help patients recover mobility and manage pain through exercises and therapy.

Healthcare Administration

Manage hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities, ensuring smooth operations and compliance.
